At PHOTOGRAPHY by DESIGN we offer you only the best in quality and service for all of your photographic needs. Most of our wedding packages include engagement and bridal sessions, although we will custom design your photographic package to fit your special needs.

All previews (proofs) are yours to keep. We are on location ready to photograph your special day as you dress and we stay with you until you and your spouse leave your reception; therefore we will schedule only one wedding coverage per day. Wedding albums offered in any option will include the photographs that will fill that page. The photographs and sizes can be chosen by you after viewing your proof album.

Your wedding will be photographed in both color and black and white with proofs from both included in your proof album. All of our photographers and their assistants wear tuxedos. We use medium format cameras, professional lighting and always have back-up equipment. We will gladly be available for appointments to give you the time to look over our work without any obligations. Please call to schedule your appointment, day or evenings are available. Should you have any questions pertaining to your wedding photography, feel free to call or email us.
