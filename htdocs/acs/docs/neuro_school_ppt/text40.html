Brain Fingerprinting Application

  • A suspect had been a person of interest in an unsolved murder case for 15 years. The P-3 wave showed the record stored in his brain matched critical details of the crime scene only the offender could know.

  • The suspect pled guilty in exchange for life in prison without the possibility of parole. He also confessed to the previously unsolved murders of three women.
