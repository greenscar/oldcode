In his consulting practice, Dr. Edgar has provided expert forensic psychiatric services on a wide variety of legal cases throughout Florida. His expert analysis and testimony has been brought to bear on medical malpractice issues, labor law, workman’s compensation cases, findings of mental capacity, estate planning, custody and criminal cases and many others. He has also devised and conducted innovative programs in organizational dynamics for major corporations and counseled businesses on such diverse programs as prevention of workplace violence and sexual harassment.
