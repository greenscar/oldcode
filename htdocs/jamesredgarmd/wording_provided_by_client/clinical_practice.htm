Dr. James R. Edgar
Dr. Edgar’s clinical practice is based on the belief that skilled
and experience psychiatrists are best able to completely and
comprehensively evaluate patient conditions and devise
treatments that work in a real-world context. The special patients
Dr. Edgar treats are generally able to continue their full and
productive lives as they improve their psycho-social health. In his
practice, psychoanalytical treatment precedes and provides a
basis for medication, if warranted.
