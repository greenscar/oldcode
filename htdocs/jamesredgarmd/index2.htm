Meet Dr. Edgar

Dr. James R. Edgar has more than 30 years’ experience in psychiatry and psychoanalysis, providing in-depth clinical care for patients and a wide range of psychiatric consulting services. In his clinical work, he specializes in evaluating and treating such high-achieving professionals as attorneys, senior business executives, clergymen and medical professionals. As the “doctor’s doctor,” he has extensive experience in working with physicians themselves and with hospitals and health maintenance and preferred provider organizations.

Dr. Edgar’s clinical practice is based on the belief that skilled and experience psychiatrists are best able to completely and comprehensively evaluate patient conditions and devise treatments that work in a real-world context. The special patients Dr. Edgar treats are generally able to continue their full and productive lives as they improve their psycho-social health. In his practice, psychoanalytical treatment precedes and provides a basis for medication, if warranted.

In his consulting practice, Dr. Edgar has provided expert forensic psychiatric services on a wide variety of legal cases throughout Florida. His expert analysis and testimony has been brought to bear on medical malpractice issues, labor law, workman’s compensation cases, findings of mental capacity, estate planning, custody and criminal cases and many others. He has also devised and conducted innovative programs in organizational dynamics for major corporations and counseled businesses on such diverse programs as prevention of workplace violence and sexual harassment. Services Provided:

  • Psychoanalysis (treatment approach)
  • Mental health and general psychology (normal and abnormal development)
  • Specialized evaluation and treatment of high-performance professionals
  • Treatment and developmental programs for couples, families and adolescents
  • Forensic consulting, case evaluation, expert testimony on a wide range of psychoanalytic issues (labor, workman’s comp, medical malpractice, family and estate law, etc.)
  • Structured administrative and organizational programs for businesses
  • Organizational dynamics for organizations of all types

About Dr. Edgar:

Dr James R. Edgar is board certified in adult psychoanalysis by the American Psychoanalytic Association and board certified in psychiatry by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology. He is past president of the Florida Psychiatric Society, founding president of the Foundation for Psychoanalysis and a distinguished fellow of the American Psychiatric Association. He serves as a consultant to the Florida Department of Labor and Employment Security, the Florida Bar Association, the Agency for Health Care Administration Medicaid Program Integrity, the State of Florida Agency for Health Care Administration and Department of Health and Hillsborough County Public Schools. He also consults with Physicians’ Recovery Network, the Impaired Practitioners Program of Florida and the Intervention Project for Nurses, The Recovery Resource for Florida Nurses. Dr. Edgar also works with the Episcopal Diocese of Southwest Florida, the Diocese of Central Florida and St. Leo Abbey, Benedictine Order, Catholic Church.

Dr. Edgar received his M.D. from Louisiana State University School of Medicine. He completed his residency in Psychiatry at the Langley Porter Neuro-Psychiatric Institute, University of California San Francisco, and was a fellow at the San Francisco Psychoanalytic Institute. He serves as Clinical Associate Professor, University of South Florida College of Medicine’s Department of Psychiatry and is Director of Continuing Medical Education with the Florida Psychiatric Society. He has been named in Woodward White’s The Best Doctors in America.
