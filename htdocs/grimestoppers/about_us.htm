A LITTLE ABOUT US

Grime stoppers was founded by three individuals who have a desire to be independent business owners. They have sometimes referred to as “clean freaks” by family and friends and decided to put our talents to work for them.

JESSICA – OWNER/DIRECTOR OF ACCOUNTING

Jessica holds a bachelor of science degree in communications from the University of Iowa. She has called Portland home since 2001. Since putting her roots down in the Rose City, Jessica has been working as an accounting specialist for a private facility. Jessica brings her “people skills” and desire to succeed to this endeavor.

PAULA – OWNER/DIRECTOR OF MARKETING

Paula holds a bachelor of science degree in health care administration from Concordia University. While attending college, Paula held positions with Nike and Interactive Systems, Inc. as an environmental specialist “janitor.” From 1995 until just recently, she has been working as the sales/marketing director for a private facility in Portland. Paula brings her great organization skills and attention to detail to this endeavor.

SARAH – OWNER/CONSULTANT

Sarah holds a bachelor of arts degree in exercise science from Portland state university. Since relocating to Portland from New York, Sarah has worked in many capacities for a private facility as well. Sarah brings her enthusiasm and eagerness to succeed to this endeavor!

ALL BRING A STRONG WORK ETHIC!
