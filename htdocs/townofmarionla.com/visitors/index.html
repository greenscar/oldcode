The Mayhaw Festival... and so much more

The Mayhaw Festival that takes place Mother's Day Weekend each year. People can come and taste the famous delicious Mayhaw jelly, jelly that tastes good on just about anything.

But if you missed this year's festival, don't worry, you can still get the jelly at A Little Bit of Everything, and yes, there is a reason for that name. Owner Peggy Rhodes says she carries just about everything. If she can find it, she'll get it. And A Little Bit of Everything isn't the only place in Marion where you will find a wide collection of antiques.

You can visit the home of Danny and Ann Smith, which they have restored, and if you are lucky, they will one day soon open it as a bed and breakfast.
So if you are looking for a good time, head to Marion where there is always room for you on Main Street.*

*Source: KARD FOX 14, "Region 10 Roadtrip", http://kard.com
