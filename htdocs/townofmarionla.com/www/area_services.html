Small Business Assistance

The LA Tech SBDC (Small Business Development Center) serves Bienville Parish.  Louisiana Tech’s Small Business Development Center is a comprehensive resource center and assistance provider for small businesses in north-central Louisiana. The SBDC provides training, counseling, and information for active and prospective entrepreneurs. By supporting the creation of new enterprises and nurturing existing business operations, the SBDC endeavors to improve the economic well being of this region and the area’s residents. Union Parish enjoys an excellent parish-supported public library system. The library is located in Farmerville, with a head librarian and a staff of six. Volumes in the library total in excess of 48,753, with approximately 120 new additions per month. The facilities offer reading and reference materials, such as periodicals, films and audio materials for all ages. A bookmobile serves outlaying communities in the parish on a regular basis. The large collections at the university libraries are available to parish residents. *
