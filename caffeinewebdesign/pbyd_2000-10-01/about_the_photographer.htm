YOUR PHOTOGRAPHER

 

B.W. (Wayne) Sandlin began as a wedding and portrait photographer in 1970 after military service. He received his Bachelor of Arts with a creative photography major in 1973. After receiving his degree he worked as a civil service photographer for the state of Louisiana and operated a bridal and portrait studio. In 1977 he moved to Houston and during the 1980's he worked as a commercial photographer doing ad campaigns for clients that included Macintosh computers, Swan Food Corp., Red Barron Pizza and local Houston accounts. During that time he shot assignments with Ultra Magazine, The Greater Conroe Chamber of Commerce Magazine and Metropolitan Beaumont Magazine.

During the 80's and 90's his black and white fine art photography began being exhibited in art galleries in the Houston area, and as always he continued his bridal and portrait photography.

With many years of experience in the photographic field, Wayne can offer you the confidence in knowing that your wedding and portrait needs will more than meet with your expectations.
