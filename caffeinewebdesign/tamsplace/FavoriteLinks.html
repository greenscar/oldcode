Yanni!

 This is the most beautiful website I've ever seen! Check out the website for this very talented musician, and his special message about learning to live with one another through tolerance. Includes links for purchasing Yanni's music (and other things), his history, tour information, photo gallery, message boards, and more.

ezone

 Some of the coolest shockwave games, developed by two brothers from Austrailia. Gorgeous animation and fun music. Family Friendly site, although most of the games are a bit advanced for the very young.

HTML Goodies This is the site where I learned just about everything I know about making websites. Great tutorials written by Joe Burns, who adds humor to what could be a very dry subject. Also includes tutorials for CGI, Java, DHTML, and much more. He also puts out a great weekly newsletter in which he critiques websites (from a developer's perspective). Very useful information! This site is my lifeline for web development.

Symphonie's Room

 Includes a great page of homeschool links for preschoolers. Most links are logo (visual) links rather than a list, for a pre-reading audience.
