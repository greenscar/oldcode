Portraits
by
Annie McLaughlin

Taught by my father to work in different mediums and with different subjects, PEOPLE have become my favorite subject of study over the years. I am inspired by the sense of spirit found in all of us, and entertained by candidness caught on film. Not unlike Norman Rockwell, who did his work from photographic reference, I feel that EVERYONE is a work of art!

The apple doesn't fall far from the tree. My father, shown in the storage room at his highschool, displays one of his first portraits, done in oils. Though he has mastered most mediums, his passion is in painting and he still paints today. Here is one of his most recent paintings!
It has been my privilage and pleasure over the years to take your favorite photos, and bring them to life through my renderings. These portraits are a one of a kind gift for yourself and others.

My portraits are done with a PHOTO REFERENCE ONLY. They are done in watercolor on watercolor paper. For pencil renditions, they are done in graphite/lead on acid-free Bristol board and treated with a non-smudge finish. Both are packaged between two foamboards in a clear plastic cover so that it can be stored easily and safely until framing.


PRICES FOR PENCIL are as follows: For one torso or shoulder and head of a person, the charge is $100. For a whole body, or intricate background, add $25. If you desire a group shot (which can be done with a single photo, or several photos), AND there are more than four people (head and torso), the price drops to $75 per person.

HERE ARE SOME HELPFUL HINTS WHEN CONSIDERING HAVING A PROTRAIT DONE:

PHOTO REFERENCE Clearness and sharpness of photo is important. (For a watercolor, a color photo is required for reference.) I can usually work with emailed copies or photo copies when the quality of the picture is maintained. The unique and candid snapshots supply me with a great deal of spirit and personality. Black and white photos are always exciting to do, but most importantly, if it is a favorite of yours, there's a good chance it will be a winner like these!

TRANSACTIONS can be done by mail or other means of shipping, especially if you are out of the Portland area.
