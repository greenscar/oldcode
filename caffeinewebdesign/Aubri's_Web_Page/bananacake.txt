Banana Nut Cake 



---------- Recipe via Meal-Master (tm) v8.02
 
      Title: BANANA NUT CAKE
 Categories: Cakes, Fruits/nuts
      Yield: 1 servings
 
      3    Mashed bananas
  1 1/2 c  Sugar
    1/2 c  Oil
      1    Egg
    1/4 c  Milk
  1 1/2 c  Flour
      1 ts Baking soda
    1/2 c  Walnuts (optional)
 
  Mix together in order given.  Pour into lightly greased and floured oblong
  or small loaf pans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
