
                          GRANADAN BAKED BANANAS

Recipe By     : 
Serving Size  : 4    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : 
  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1       c            Brown sugar
   1       c            Fresh orange juice
     1/2   c            Dark rum
     1/2   t            Cinnamon
     1/2   t            Nutmeg
   1       t            Vanilla extract
   4                    Ripe bananas, peeled &
                        -sliced lengthwise

  Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F).  Grease well a 9 inch ovenproof
  pan.  Put the brown sugar in a heavy saucepan.  Heat carefully
  until it liquifies.  Add the orange juice, rum, cinnamon, nutmeg
  and vanilla.  Boil for a few seconds.  Then, pour over the
  bananas.  Place in the oven and bake for about 30 minutes.
