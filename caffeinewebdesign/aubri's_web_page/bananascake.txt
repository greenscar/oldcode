Banana Pudding Cake



---------- Recipe via Meal-Master (tm) v8.02
 
      Title: Banana Pudding Cake
 Categories: Digest, Feb95
      Yield: 8 servings
 
    3/4 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
    3/4 c  Unbleached flour, white
      1 ts Baking soda
      1 ts Baking powder
    1/8 ts Salt
    1/2 c  Raisins
      2 ea Large ripe bananas, cut into
    1/2    Inch slices
    1/2 c  Apple juice
    1/2 c  Maple syrup
      2 ts Vanilla extract
 
  With Pam, spritz and flour a 1 qt (6 cup) bundt pan. Mix dry ingredients in
  a bowl, and slowly add bananas, ensuring that they are coated with flour on
  both sides. Combine liquids, and add to flour, mixing evenly. Pour into
  bundt pan, and cover tightly with foil.
  
  Using trivit in pressure cooker, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Lower
  bundt pan into cooker, cover, lock, and immediately bring to high pressure.
  Cook for 35 minutes. At end, quick release pressure, remove bundt pan, and
  place on wire rack to cool. Unmold and serve.
