The Company

PHOTOGRAPHY by DESIGN, located in Houston, North off I-10 East just off of Belt Way 8, is a full service wedding and portrait studio that offers you only the best in quality and service for all of your photographic needs. Our studios unique concept provides one photographer capturing all formal and candid images in color, with an assistant for second lighting, and a second photographer capturing artistic black and whites. Most of our wedding packages include engagement or bridal portrait sessions, although we will custom design your photographic package to fit your special needs. All preview (proofs) are always yours to keep, and your wedding day prints will be available for viewing by you and your guest on the Internet.
 
We will be on location ready to photograph your special dayas you dress and we will stay with you until you and your spouse leave your reception, because of this service we will schedule only one wedding coverage per day. Wedding albums offered in any option will include the photographs that will fill the pages; you choose the photographs and sizes after you’ve had time to view your proof album. All of our staff wear tuxedos at your wedding and reception.
 
We use professional format cameras, professional lighting and always have back-up equipment. We will gladly be available for appointments to give you the time to look over our work without any obligations. Please call to schedule your appointment; day and evenings are available. Always feel free to call or E-mail us with any questions that you may have pertaining to your photographic needs.

 

 

The Photographer

 

B.W. (Wayne) Sandlin began as a wedding and portrait photographer in 1970 after military service and received his Bachelor of Arts in creative photography from Northeast Louisiana University in 1973. After graduating, he worked as a civil service photographer for the state of Louisiana and operated a bridal and portrait studio. In 1977 he moved to Houston and in the early 80’s he worked on his Master of Fine Art at the University of Houston. He is a member of the Professional Photographers of America, the Professional Photographers Guild of Houston and the Texas Professional Photographers Association, Inc.
 
Throughout the 80’s and 90’s his black and white fine art photography began being exhibited in art galleries in the Houston area and, as always, he continued his bridal and portrait photography.
 
With may years of experience in the photographic field, Wayne can offer you the confidence in knowing that your wedding and portrait needs will more than meet with your expectations.
