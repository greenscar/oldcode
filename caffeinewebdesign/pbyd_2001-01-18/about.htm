The Company

PHOTOGRAPHY by DESIGN is a full service wedding and portrait studio that offers you only the best in quality and service for all of your photographic needs.Wayne Sandlin, the owner, is a member of the Professional Photographers of America and the Professional Photographers Guild of Houston. Our studios unique concept provides one photographer capturing all formals and candids in color and a second photographer capturing artistic black and whites.
 
Most of our wedding packages include engagement or bridal portraits sessions, although we will custom design your photographic package to fit your special needs. All previews (proofs) are always yours to keep. We will be on location ready to photograph your special day as you dress and we will stay with you until you and your spouse leave your reception; because of this we will schedule only one wedding coverage per day. Wedding albums offered in any option will include the photographs that will fill the pages. You chose the photographs and sizes after you've had the time to view your proof album. All of our staff wear tuxedos at your wedding and reception. We use medium format cameras, professional lighting and always have back up equipment.
 
We will gladly be available for appointments to give you the time to look over our work without any obligations. Please call to schedule your appointment, day or evenings are available. Please feel free to call or E-mail us with any questions that your may have pertaining to your photographic needs.

 

 

The Photographer

 

B.W. (Wayne) Sandlin began as a wedding and portrait photographer in 1970 after military service and received his Bachelor of Arts in creative photography in 1973. After graduating, he worked as a civil service photographer for the state of Louisiana and operated a bridal and portrait studio. In 1977 he moved to Houston and worked during the 1980's as a commercial photographer doing ad campaigns for clients that included Macintosh computers, Swan Food Corp., Red Baron Pizza and other local Houston accounts. During that time he also shot assignments with Ultra Magazine, The Greater Conroe Chamber of Commerce Magazine and Metropolitan Beaumont Magazine.
 
Throughout the 80's and 90's his black and white fine art photography began being exhibited in art galleries in the Houston area and, as always, he continued his bridal and portrait photography.
 
With many years of experience in the photographic field, Wayne can offer you the confidence in knowing that your wedding and portrait needs will more than meet with your expectations.
