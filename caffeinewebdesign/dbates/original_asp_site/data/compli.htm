Business Solutions Seminar Series

Durham and Bates is partnering with COMPLI (formerly Polis) to provide a business solutions seminar series. COMPLI pioneers web-based workplace compliance solutions, and helps employers address the time/cost burden and legal liability associated with local, state and federal employment law compliance. The company's HRProact System provides businesses the legal information and technological infrastructure essential to efficiently manage workplace compliance issues and streamline administrative workflow. The HRProact System will allow human resources departments to customize, record, store, modify, distribute, update and track all information related to employment, including personnel policies and procedures, supervisory and HR guidebooks, training programs, and all corporate forms related to these functions.

Durham and Bates believes the distinction of COMPLI's program is its new level of interactivity, allowing employees more control in the administration and authorization of their employment-related documentation. We've worked with COMPLI to present a Fall 2001 seminar series to our customers and other business partners. We believe ideal firms for COMPLI are those that employ more than 50; are technologically savvy, and diligent about employment practices liability.
