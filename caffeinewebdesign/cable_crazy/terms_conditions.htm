They will be able to determine whether the item is defective before shipping. Then make an online return merchandise authorization (RMA) request. See our Return Policy for more details.

All products sold at Cablecrazy.com carry a 30-Day warranty against defects due to manufacturing or failure. Warranty does not include or cover products used for overclocking, custom modifications, improper voltage, alterations, or any use of the product that does not fall into the designed use as inteded by the manufacturer. Software is not returnable or exchangeable once opened, or if the serial number or registration number has been removed. Opened software includes, but is not limited to, removing the shrink wrap from the software or opening the seal on the envelope.

Certain parts may come with extended Manufacturer's Warranty. The equipment manufacturer honors this warranty and Cable Crazy assumes no responsibility in honoring this warranty. Customer assumes all shipping costs to manufacturers.

Warranty Forfeiture
Warranty is not granted if a product is received without original invoice. Warranty is void if serial numbers, product stickers, or manufacturer seals have been removed, altered or tampered with. Warranty is void if mishandling, improper use, or defacing of the product that would normally void manufacturer's warranty has occurred. Products damaged due to improper or inadequate packaging when returned for RMA purposes are not granted warranty coverage.

-:- Shipping Policy

Cable Crazy ships each order via UPS or USPS. Rates are automatically calculated by UPS or USPS Online Shipping Calculator by accordance of weight.

Cable Crazy will ship your order within 1-3 business days. If for some reason an item is out of stock and Cable Crazy cannot provide the item when your order is placed Cable Crazy will give you the option of a refund for the items out of stock or will provide you with an ETA.

Shipping charges will be added to your order upon check out. Since shipping cost is based on region, charges cannot be figured until the bill to and ships to portions of the order form are filled out. Shipping information is displayed immediately following the bill to and ship to information in the check out process. This allows you to view shipping fees before any charges are placed to your credit card. Shipping charges are not included in the price of the item. Shipping time is not guaranteed; shipping cost is also not guaranteed. We are sorry, but we can not refund the shipping charges for weather related delays.

Cable Crazy does not take responsibility for delayed arrivals on packages due to reasons beyond our control such as Weather, train delays, rural shipping areas, or if a package does not arrive at the scheduled delivery date as a result of the customer not being at the shipping location if a signature is required.

Cable Crazy requires a signature for orders valued at $100 or more. No exceptions!

Damaged Items
All UPS and USPS packages are fully insured. If you receive a damaged package, you must notify us immediately by phone 1-(888)-509-5903. Sending back damaged merchandise voids the shipping insurance and no credit will be given. Damaged packages must be held for seven days with original packaging for shipper inspection.

DOA
Any product found to be Dead on Arrival (DOA) will be replaced within 10 business days, as long as the item is in stock, and is still an inventoried item. All DOA items must be brought to our attention within 3 business days of receiving your product. An RMA number is required. See RMA policies for further information. Qualified technicians will test and determine all DOA’s before issuing a replacement.
